Protect Your Workforce,
Protect Your Business

The new OSHWC rule has made annual health check-ups mandatory for employees above 40 years, focusing on preventive healthcare and early disease detection. This initiative promotes a healthier, more productive workforce while reducing occupational risks.

Industries where health check-ups are mandatory:

  1. Factories & Hazardous Industries
  2. Mines
  3. Docks
  4. Construction Sites
  5. Plantations

Key Aspects of the Rule

  • Eligibility: Employees above 40 years qualify for free annual health check-ups.
  • Coverage: Blood tests, lung function, vision & hearing tests, and risk-based screenings.
  • Employer Responsibility: Provide check-ups at no cost & maintain confidential records.
  • Benefits: Early detection, reduced absenteeism, improved productivity & well-being.

The rule aims to establish a culture of preventive healthcare and strengthen overall organizational health.

Implementing OSHWC Compliance

  1. Risk Assessment: Identify workplace health hazards.
  2. Health Plan: Tailor annual check-ups based on industry risks.
  3. Medical Officer: Partner with certified providers.
  4. Employee Communication: Clearly explain benefits.
  5. Record Maintenance: Secure medical reports.
  6. Preventive Measures: Act on findings.
  7. Review & Update: Improve continuously.
